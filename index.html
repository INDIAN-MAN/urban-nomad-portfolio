Concept

Urban Nomad

Showcasing resilience and individuality through contemporary streetwear.

  • Editorial: Focus on movement and texture in concrete urban environments.
  • E‑commerce: Clean, accurate representation of fit and material.

Visual Direction

  • High‑contrast street photography
  • Gritty film grain aesthetics
  • Strong architectural lines
  • Inspired by Hypebeast + high‑end sneaker campaigns

Photoshoot Plan

Location

Lighting

  • DSLR/Mirrorless camera
  • 24–70mm lens
  • Off‑camera flash
  • Light stand
  • High contrast, desaturated tones
  • Film grain
  • Sharpened textures
  • Subtle vignetting
